IEEE Student Branch
IEEE Student Branch VVITU is a student-driven community that promotes innovation, technical learning, and professional development at Vasireddy Venkatadri International Technological University. It organizes workshops, competitions, and networking events to help students gain hands-on experience, enhance skills beyond academics, and connect with the global IEEE community.
